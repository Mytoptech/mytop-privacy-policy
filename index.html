Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2025

MYHOTPICKS is a mobile application designed to allow users to participate in beauty contest rankings, vote for candidates, build custom Top 10 lists, and join community-driven competitions.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data. The application is developed and maintained by Antonio Carmona Polanco.

1. Information We Collect

When you create a MYHOTPICKS account, we collect the following information:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Age
  • Country of residence
  • Profile Image (if provided)
  • Password (only for email/password sign-ups; not stored in plain text)
  • Google Account Data (only basic profile information if using Google Sign-In)

We also store user-generated data, including:

  • Your Top 10 candidate rankings for different pageants (Miss Universe, Miss Grand, etc.)
  • Points awarded during votes
  • Messages left during paid or free votes
  • Communities you follow or create

2. How We Use Your Data

Your personal data is used solely for the purposes of operating and improving the app, including:

  • Displaying your rankings and preferences in the app
  • Generating global statistics and community-based leaderboards
  • Personalizing your experience (e.g., showing country flags, calculating user influence)
  • Tracking voting history and enabling users to support their favorite candidates
  • Creating and managing private or public communities

We do not sell or share your personal data with any third parties.

3. Authentication & Google Sign-In

You may sign in using your email/password or through your Google account. If using Google Sign-In, we only access:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your profile picture (optional)

This data is used to automatically create your MYHOTPICKS profile.

4. User Messaging

MYHOTPICKS includes a private messaging feature that allows users to communicate directly with each other. Messages may include text content shared voluntarily by users as part of conversations or community interactions.

All messages are:

  • Stored securely in Firebase Cloud Firestore
  • Encrypted in transit using HTTPS
  • Not shared with third parties or used for advertising

We do not read, moderate, or use user messages for analytics or profiling purposes, except when required to respond to user reports or enforce our Terms of Service.

5. Advertisements

MYHOTPICKS uses Google AdMob to serve advertisements. These ads may use anonymized identifiers (e.g., advertising ID) to personalize ad content. No personally identifiable information is used or shared for advertising purposes.

6. Use of Device Camera

MYHOTPICKS may request access to your device’s camera in order to allow users to upload a profile image directly from the app. This functionality is entirely optional. The camera is never used in the background, and no images or videos are taken without your explicit interaction and consent.

No camera content is shared with third parties. All usage is local and secure.

7. Data Security

We take data security seriously. All user data is securely stored using:

  • Firebase Authentication for managing user accounts and credentials
  • Firebase Cloud Firestore for storing user profiles, rankings, messages, and votes

Passwords are never stored in plain text and access to your data is restricted and monitored.

8. Data Retention

We retain user data for as long as your account remains active. If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed from our systems, including rankings, votes, messages, and community participation.

9. Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access the data we have about you
  • Correct any inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

You can manage most of these actions directly in the app under your profile settings.

10. Children’s Privacy

MYHOTPICKS is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we learn that we have unintentionally collected data from a child, we will promptly delete it.

11. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. You will be notified of any significant changes via in-app notification or email.
